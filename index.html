About Me

Before web development, I spent several years as a pharmacist working in the retail setting. After a few online classes, I realized I really enjoyed coding and using code to solve problems. I decided to take the leap and enroll in a bootcamp full-time. I graduated from Digital Crafts full-stack immersive course in December, and I was honored when I was chosen to act as a developer in residence (kind of like a teaching assistant) for the January 2020 cohort.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy traveling. Some of my favorite places are Montreal, Berlin, and Mexico City. While in town, you can find me going out to check out different restaurants or staying home and baking. Either way, I always like to try something new!
